WHO ARE WE

Talisha is an indian private space research organization.Talisha was established in 2024.Talisha working in reusable rocket, cube Satellite and Space Exploration. Talisha's aim is to develop our india in the field of reusable space tech. In Our Space Research Organization Collaboratively Working with Students and Mentors. Student Make his own Creative and Innovative Solutions in Space Technology and Competition with Global Challenges.Our Organization try to Solve the Global space Problems and Make low cost payloads.To make space exploration accessible and affordable for everyone, especially the Startup and research community.
